HighNotes

Minimalists Micro-Journaling App

Personal journal is a perfect place for you to think, feel, discover, expand, remember, and dream. The age we're living in is overloaded with information. Sometimes it gets so noisy it's hard to hear your own voice. Journaling is all about taking a break, turning your attention inwards and listen to what is going on in your mind.

HighNotes is a beautifully designed micro-journaling app for capturing and saving your thoughts, ideas or memories. It's also a great place for your favorite quotes and excerpts from the books you're reading. Your space for retrospecting and observing the contents of your mind.

HighNotes is designed to be clean and minimalistic to bring focus on the words you fill it with. It's AI based algorithm is sensitive to the contents of each entry you add and it will layout them beautifully in an engaging feed.

If you journal on a daily basis, going back to your previous messages and navigating through and searching your entries is just a few taps away with HighNotes powerful search tool.

HighNotes let's you share your posts as images on Instagram or any other sharing option on your iPhone. You can also save it to your Camera Roll, edit or delete previously created posts.
